The Smart Suburbanite helps everyday homeowners make practical decisions about smart home, energy-saving, security, and comfort upgrades without needing to become tech experts.

This site is for 40+ English-speaking suburban homeowners who want clearer, calmer guidance before buying or setting up home technology.

The goal is to publish plain-English guides, checklists, comparison frameworks, and buying questions that help readers understand tradeoffs before spending money.
